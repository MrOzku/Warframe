Warframe is a third-person co-op focused action shooter.
The game situates the players as members of the Tenno race,
newly awoken from centuries of cryosleep to fight
the Grineer, Corpus and to stop the infestation.

To progress in the game your main objective is to
do missions on different planets to get coordinates
to other planets. You can also craft weapons
and Warframes from the resources you find.
