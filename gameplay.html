After you wake up from centuries of cryosleep
a Grineer General, Captain Vor, tries to control you.
The first objetive in the game is to escape from
Captain Vor, get some equipment and the defeat him.
You'll be guided by a former sentient called Lotus
and a cephalon called Ordis.

This is just the tutorial.

After this you are
pretty much on your own
but Ordis can give you some
tutorials on how everything works.
You can do solo mission or
play co-op with you friends or
just random other players.

There are also many quests you
can do to learn more about
the story and the history of this world.
